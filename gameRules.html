Synopsys

Boris finds himself in an amusement park in the middle of a zombie apocalypse. He is attacked by an army of zombies and fights for his survival with cotton candy.

Objective

The goal of the game is to kill as many zombies as possible by shooting cotton candy at them by aiming and shooting with the mouse. The player can dodge/flee from the zombies by moving with the ZQSD keys.
